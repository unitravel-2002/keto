About Us

Welcome to the Blue Sea Hotel.

The Blue Sea Hotel is located at 180 An Duong Vuong Street, Nguyen Van Cu Ward, Vietnam. It is located in the center of Quy Nhon city, as well as near the main road, Nguyen Tat Thanh Street, a crowded area with a sea view opposite the hotel.


With 4-star international quality, the Blue Sea Hotel not only perfectly meets your resting needs but also brings wonderful moments of relaxation and entertainment and leaves an impressive cultural aftertaste in the cuisine here. The hotel is a harmonious combination of luxurious and modern style, including a restaurant complex, dining area, and event organization gathered on a campus of nearly 250 m2.


The Blue Sea Hotel consists of 4 floors for accommodation, 1 ground floor for dining and event organization, and 1 basement floor for parking. The four accommodation floors of the hotel include 41 rooms for three basic room types: standard rooms on the 1st and 3rd floors, superior rooms on the 2nd floor, and deluxe rooms on the 4th floor.

The check-in and check-out areas

The front desk of the Blue Sea hotel is situated right by the hotel entrance, near the pathways leading to the staircase. It typically features a representative counter staffed 24/7 to welcome guests, offer farewell, and handle all guest feedback.

The dining area

It is located to the right of the front desk area, making it convenient for serving breakfast and meals throughout the day.

The wedding and event hall

It is located to the left of the front desk area, is elegantly designed and flexible to accommodate special events.

The spacious area, with high ceilings and natural light, creates an airy and warm atmosphere.

A professional team is available to organize and cater to various events, from weddings and conferences to birthdays and other private gatherings.
